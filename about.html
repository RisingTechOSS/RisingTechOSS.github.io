About RisingOS


RisingOS is a custom firmware built on the Android Open Source Project (AOSP), designed to enhance the Android experience. Our mission is to bring OEM-like customizations while maintaining the stability and performance you expect from a custom ROM. RisingOS combines the best of legacy features with modern improvements, delivering a seamless and smooth user experience for all devices.

Our Core Values


We are committed to the principles of openness, transparency, and innovation. RisingOS is built by the community, for the community. We believe that technology should be accessible, flexible, and empowering, which is why we focus on providing users with full control over their devices.

The RisingOS Story


RisingOS began as a passion project to offer a reliable, customizable, and feature-rich alternative to stock Android. Over the years, we've refined the system to meet the needs of power users and enthusiasts, while maintaining a focus on stability and performance.
